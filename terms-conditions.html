Punjab Construction designs and executes builds to strict structural benchmarks (including Grade-C50 concrete and Seismic Class A structural checks). However, you acknowledge the following client responsibilities:

  3. Site Clearances: Ensure safe, unobstructed access to the layout site for heavy machinery (e.g., concrete pumps, excavators, crane masts).
