About Punjab Construction

About Company

Leading Civil Construction in India

Punjab Construction is a leading civil construction company in India, renowned for delivering high-quality infrastructure projects since 2022.

With a strong foundation in engineering excellence, we specialize in a wide range of construction services, including residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability has earned us a solid reputation in the construction industry.

Founded in 2022, On Engineers and Punjab Construction secured its first construction contract with Jindal Steels in Orissa. Since then, the company has expanded into Punjab, successfully undertaking a pipe rack and building project for IOL Pharmaceuticals and Chemicals Limited in Barnala.
